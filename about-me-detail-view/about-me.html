Who am I?

I am a passionated, eager to learn and self taught iOS Developer with a very wide range of different skills across multiple segments, such as in Engineering, videography, science and electronics with both hardware and software knowledge.

This interest in different fields started at a very young age, when I was just 5 years old and I got my very first dirt bike. And with it came my interest in mechanical engineering.

This actually kickstarted my career as a car mechanic at the Audi dealer (for 11 years), eventually I felt like I could learn more and pursue my interest in electronics, so I decided to make a career switch. To do so I started studying at HAN University of Applied Sciences. During my studies I stumbled on the job as electronical prototype developer at Autodidact which I am working for over 4,5 years now. Also around the same period I started my own YouTube channel where I modify (mostly the interior of) my car.

I started my iOS journey back in 2018, while I was still working at Autodidact. Because they wanted to create an iOS app for the EuroTech Center (Automotive) Helpdesk (which is a different department of Autodidact itself).

At the same time I was looking for a graduation assignment for the Bachelor Automotive Engineering. So we decided to combine the two and as result I created the iOS app: ETCA "EuroTech Center App", which combines automotive engineering and iOS Development.

I received an 9 for this graduation assignment and graduated with honors! I really learned a lot creating ETCA and immediately fell in love with iOS Development. When SwiftUI came out I immediately started practicing with this new declarative user interface toolkit. Which resulted in my two latest created apps Mi Shopping List & eXandOs.
