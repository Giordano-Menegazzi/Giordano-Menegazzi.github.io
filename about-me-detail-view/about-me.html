Background

I am a passionated, eager to learn and self taught iOS Developer with a very wide range of different skills across multiple segments, such as in Engineering, videography and electronics with both hardware and software knowledge.

This interest in different fields started at a very young age, when I was just 5 years old I got my very first dirt bike and with it came my interest in mechanical engineering.

This was the beginning of my career as a car mechanic, where I worked my way up to become one of the few qualified Audi technicians allowed to work on sports cars such as the Audi R8. Eventually I felt like I could learn even more and wanted to pursue my interest in electronics, so I decided to start studying next to my full-time job as car technician.

I started studying at HAN University of Applied Sciences Automotive Engineering. During my studies I stumbled up on my current job as "Electronical" Prototype Developer at Autodidact which I am working for over 4,5 years now.

I started my iOS journey way back in 2018, while I was still studying and working for Autodidact. Because Autodidact wanted to create an iOS app for the EuroTech Center "Automotive" Helpdesk (which is a different department of Autodidact itself).

At the same time I was looking for a graduation assignment for the Bachelor Automotive Engineering. So we decided to combine the two and as a result I created the iOS app: ETCA "EuroTech Center App" for Autodidact, which combines Automotive Engineering and iOS Development.

I actually recieved a 9 for this graduation assignment and graduated with honors! I really learned a lot creating ETCA and immediately fell in love with native iOS Development. When SwiftUI came out I immediately started practicing with this new declarative user interface toolkit. Which resulted in my two latest created apps Mi Shopping List & eXandOs.

I enjoy every aspect of iOS development and try to follow and learn as much about it as I can. As a result of this growing passion with native iOS Development. I decided to make another career switch and become a full-time iOS Developer in a professional team of iOS Developers, with the same passion as mine and can't wait to further improve my skills there.
