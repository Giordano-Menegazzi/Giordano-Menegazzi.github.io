Background

With this dirt bike came my interest in mechanical engineering. This actually kickstarted my career as a car mechanic, where I quickly worked my way up to become one of the few qualified Audi technicians that is allowed to work on sports cars, such as the Audi R8!

Eventually I felt like I could learn even more and wanted to pursue my interest in electronics, so I decided to start studying next to my full-time job as technician.

I started studying at HAN University of Applied Sciences Automotive Engineering in Arnhem.

During my studies I stumbled up on my current job as "Electronical" Prototype Developer at Autodidact where I'm working for over 4,5 years now.

I actually started my iOS journey way back in 2018, while I was still studying and working for Autodidact. Because Autodidact wanted to create an iOS app for the 'Automotive' Helpdesk EuroTech Center (which is a different department of Autodidact itself).

At the same time I was looking for a graduation assignment for my B.S. So we decided to combine the two and as a result I created the iOS app ETCA 'EuroTech Center App', which combines Automotive Engineering and iOS Development.

I actually recieved a 9 for this graduation assignment and graduated with honors (Cum laude)! I really learned a lot creating ETCA and immediately fell in love with native iOS Development in Swift.

When SwiftUI came out I immediately started practicing with this new declarative user interface toolkit. Which resulted in my two latest created iOS apps Mi Shopping List & eXandOs.

I actually enjoy every aspect of iOS development from designing to programming and always try to learn as much as possible along the way.

As a result of this growing passion with native iOS Development. I decided to make a career switch and become a full-time iOS Developer in a professional team of iOS Developers, with the same passion as mine and can't wait to further improve and share my skills there!
